About me

I am a second-year Computer Science student at the University of California, Los Angeles (UCLA). My academic focus revolves around AI/ML, computer security, and advanced mathematics, supported by coursework in advanced data structures, probability, computer architecture, and linear algebra. Beyond academics, I am actively involved as an executive board member of Beta Theta Pi and a proud alum of Bellarmine College Preparatory.

I have diverse software engineering experience encompassing both front-end and back-end applications. Looking ahead, my ambition is to specialize further in back-end design and delve into the intricate world of AI/ML architecture, aiming to contribute significantly to the field's innovation and development.

Outside of my professional pursuits, my interests include traveling, having explored over 35 countries across every continent. I'm also an avid 49ers fan, enjoy golfing, and dedicate time to bodybuilding, maintaining my physique in my spare time.
